The Trinity wallet is a new, user-friendly wallet for IOTA. IOTA is a distributed ledger technology that is feeless, decentralised, and scalable.

Application features include:

- State-of-the-art security
- Multi-account management
- Pricing and chart data
- Biometric login
- Automatic node selection
- Auto-reattachment and promotion

For help, please refer to https://docs.iota.org/trinity
